This website is created for a map called Rise, which is played in Warcraft 3.
The map is created by Rel!chunter, and is a dungeon crawl type of game.
In this map you will have the opportunity to pick between a wide variety of heroes and fight numerous bosses that requires the use of strategy
and teamwork to overcome. The map also holds a lot of different secrets and a vast number of items that can be combined to achieve even greater strength.

You can join the discord to find people to play with or to join in on discussions with other members. It is also here you can communicate directly with the developer, report bugs or give feedback.
